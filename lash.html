Eyelash Extension Care Tips

To keep your lash extensions looking beautiful:

  • Avoid getting them wet for the first 24 hours
  • Use oil-free makeup remover and cleansers
  • Brush them gently with a clean spoolie daily
  • Avoid rubbing or pulling on your lashes
  • Book refills every 2-3 weeks for best results
