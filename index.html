Welcome.

estiq

Estiq is a beauty salon in Joo Chiat offering facials, nail services, eyelash extensions, and scalp deep cleaning.

Welcome to estiq, your one-stop destination for beauty and wellness. Our team of highly skilled and experienced beauty specialists is passionate about helping you look and feel your absolute best.

Our studio is a cozy and inviting space that's designed to provide a relaxing and comfortable atmosphere for our clients. We use the latest equipment and high-quality products to ensure exceptional results every time.

We offer a comprehensive range of services, including nail care (manicures, pedicures, gel nails, nail art), eyelash extensions, eyebrow services, facial treatments, hair removal, and rejuvenating head spa treatments. Our team stays up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in the beauty industry to ensure that we're always providing you with the best service possible.

We believe that our clients deserve the best, and that's why we're committed to providing exceptional customer service. From the moment you walk into our studio to the time you leave, we'll make sure that you feel comfortable, pampered, and satisfied with your experience.

Thank you for choosing estiq. We can't wait to help you express yourself with beautiful, personalized beauty services!
